Homeless Encampments & Social Determinants of Health (San Diego)

Tract-level analysis • Privacy-preserving aggregation • ACS + census geography
  • Encampment counts are aggregated to census tract for safety/anonymity (no point-level locations)
  • Merged tract outcomes with ACS indicators using a tract identifier join (e.g., GEOID)
  • Outputs an analysis-ready tract table for descriptives and modeling
